**Mitch Prinstein** is the John Van Seters Distinguished Professor of Psychology and the director of clinical psychology at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. He and his research have been featured in the _New York Times_ , the _Wall Street Journal_ , National Public Radio, the _Los Angeles Times_ , CNN, _U.S. News & World Report_, _Time_ , _New York_ , _Newsweek_ , Reuters, _Family Circle_ , _Real Simple_ , and elsewhere.
